Stuff I have done
During daylight hours i work on climate at Stripe and Frontier.

In the pervious lives I have design at Carbon Direct, designed creator tools at Spotify, and helped improve core consumer experiences at Airbnb and DoorDash

On the other side, I am usually either spending time in the great outdoors or working to green the web. I am an alum of the Earth 1.0 cohort at The Strange Foundation and I helped build community through Climate Designers.

On climate, I have been a part of the solar powered media project. Most recently i wrote a foreword for the book on Sustainable Web Design.
I am Rachel, a designer in Brooklyn who loves taking care of our planet, being in motion and connecting with people through food.
